RECENT READS/WRITINGS

A few particularly enjoyable works:
  • If On a Winter's Night a Traveller by Italo Calvino
    • A unique piece of recursive literature, calling into question the role of the reader in the novel.
  • American Pastoral by Phillip Roth
    • Roth's most well-known novel, about the collapse of one man's domestic American dream
  • Zeitoun by Dave Eggers
    • Sort of a revamp of What is the What, but nonetheless a well written novel about human strength and resilience.
  • The Savage Detectives by Roberto Bolaño
    • A novel about Mexico City poets, told from the perspectives of over fifty characters.

  • The Dinner by Herman Koch
    • Fun and dark story centered around a tension-filled dinner between two brothers and their spouses.
